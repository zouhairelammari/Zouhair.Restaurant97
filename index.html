Moroccan couscous at Zouhair Restaurant

Moroccan couscous at Zouhair Restaurant is a popular traditional dish in Moroccan cuisine. Couscous consists of semolina grains, vegetables, and meat or chicken, and is cooked in a special tool called "couskas", which is a two-layered container.

Moroccan pastilla at Zouhair Restaurant

Moroccan pastilla at Zouhair Restaurant, also known as "pastela", is one of the most popular traditional dishes in Moroccan cuisine. Pastilla is a sumptuous dish consisting of layers of fluffy dough and a savory filling, usually chicken or pigeon, with nuts and spices.
